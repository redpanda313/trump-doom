# TRUMP DOOM: The Road to the Oval

**Working Title:** *Trump Doom*  
**Subtitle:** *Make America Great Again… One Debate at a Time*  
**Genre:** First-person corridor shooter (Doom-inspired) with rhetoric combat  
**Platform:** Browser (desktop-first; mobile stretch)  
**Engine:** **Classic 2.5D raycaster** (Canvas) — Doom/Wolf DNA  
**Tone:** **Mythic self-legend** — Trump as Doomguy of destiny; politics as dungeon flavor  
**Status:** Vision **LOCKED** 2026-07-15 — see [`VISION_LOCK.md`](./VISION_LOCK.md)  
**Defeat fantasy:** Zero blood — enemies **JOIN THE TRUMP-TRAIN!**  
**Protagonist:** Always adult Legend Donald (childhood = plaques only)  
**Audio:** Synth rock  
**v1 scope:** Vertical slice (Ep 0) first; wishlist: leaderboard, local co-op, local vs

---

## 1. Elevator Pitch

You are **Donald J. Trump** — always the suited figure of destiny (childhood only in lore plaques). Golden ambition. Comb-over of fate. Dream: **the Oval Office**. The world has been overrun by Narrative Demons: **Karens**, **Libtards**, **Woke Mobs**, and bureaucratic horrors. Your weapons are not bullets — **Debate**, **Framing**, **Logic**, **Facts**, **Charisma**, **The Wall** (of text). Convert foes to the **Trump-Train** (no blood). Read **story plaques**. Upgrade rhetoric. Defeat Autopen, Election Fraud, Rogue Judges. **Claim destiny.**

## 2. Core Fantasy & Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Doom DNA** | Tight corridors, map secrets, keycards, health/ammo pickups, big bosses, run-and-gun pace |
| **Rhetoric Combat** | All “attacks” are non-violent speech acts with flashy VFX (speech bubbles, gavel waves, truth lasers) |
| **Satirical Story** | Plaques, cutscenes, and enemy banter tell a tongue-in-cheek origin myth of a boy → president |
| **Progression Depth** | Weapon upgrades, loadout slots, argument styles (melee vs ranged, boss-busters vs crowd-control) |
| **Readable Arcade** | Bold silhouettes, punchy audio stingers, clear feedback — funny first, readable always |

### What This Game Is Not
- Not a realistic political sim  
- Not a shooter with real firearms or gore  
- Not a partisan lecture — **arcade satire** first

## 3. Narrative Overview

### 3.1 Logline
Donald J. Trump — already the man of legend in form — walks out of Queens destiny into an America invaded by Narrative Demons. Armed with mouth, mind, and brand, he clears districts of spin, defeats the Autopen, exposes Election Fraud, out-argues Rogue Judges, converts the chaotic to the **Trump-Train**, and claims the Oval Office.

### 3.2 Acts Structure

#### ACT I — *Queens of Destiny* (Tutorial + Early Game)
Young Donald leaves the family real-estate basement after hearing a mysterious radio: *“Someone has to fix this country…”*  
He fights early mobs (Karens, Late-Night Hosts) in suburban mazes and news studios.  
**Theme:** Ambition awakens.

#### ACT II — *The Media Labyrinth* (Mid Game)
Corridors of cable news, campus quads, and bureaucratic offices. Woke Mobs swarm; Facts and Framing become essential.  
Mid-bosses: **Cancel Culture Hydra**, **Fact-Checker Golem**.  
**Theme:** Control the narrative or be controlled by it.

#### ACT III — *Deep State Depths* (Late Mid)
Underground server farms, sealed courtrooms, ballot warehouses of pure metaphor.  
Bosses: **Autopen**, **Election Fraud**, **Rogue Judge Tribunal**.  
**Theme:** Institutions vs individual will.

#### ACT IV — *The Road to the Oval* (Endgame)
White House lawn transformed into a gauntlet of debates. Final boss multi-phase: **The Swamp / Collective Narrative**.  
Epilogue: inauguration plaque reading room — optional true ending if all plaques collected.  
**Theme:** Destiny claimed.

### 3.3 Player Character — Always Legend Donald

**Iconography rule (locked):** Like classical depictions that show the sacred child as a small adult — Donald is **always** adult Legend form in gameplay. Childhood is told only through plaques.

| Layer | Visual |
|-------|--------|
| **Default** | Navy/black suit, red tie, gold cufflinks, FPS hands |
| **Cosmetics (optional later)** | Builder hard-hat, rally eagle cape, gold-trim suit — same adult body |
| **Lore only** | Boyhood deals, radio prophecy, basement ambition (plaques) |

**Personality in-game:** Boastful one-liners, mid-combat quips, secret-room admiration (“Best plaque. Tremendous plaque.”).

### 3.4 Story Plaques (Environmental Lore)

Plaques are **wall-mounted gold plates** the player can interact with (E / click). Each adds to the **Lore Codex**. Collecting all plaques unlocks **True Ending: “The People’s Inauguration.”**

**Example plaques (partial list — full list in §8):**

1. *“I was always going to be big. The buildings just had to catch up.”* — Childhood basement  
2. *“They said ‘you can’t say that.’ So I said it louder.”* — Media Labyrinth  
3. *“The Autopen never sleeps. Neither does ambition.”* — Deep State Depths  
4. *“Fraud is a maze. Truth is a bulldozer.”* — Ballot Catacombs  
5. *“A judge without the people is just a gavel in the dark.”* — Tribunal Halls  
6. *“The Oval is not a room. It is a finish line.”* — Final approach  

Tone target: **self-mythologizing + arcade absurdity**, not documentary.

- Media distortion as dungeon fog

- Debate as DPS

## 5. Combat System — “Rhetoric Warfare”

All combat is **non-lethal**. Enemies are “defeated” by being **debunked**, **framed**, **logically collapsed**, or **overwhelmed by charisma** — then they **JOIN THE TRUMP-TRAIN!**

**Defeat VFX (locked):** red-hat confetti, gold sparkles, optional mini-train whistle; floater text **“JOINED THE TRUMP-TRAIN!”** (variants: “ALL ABOARD!”, “CONVERTED!”, “TREMENDOUS RECRUIT!”). Zero blood. No corpses.

### 5.1 Player Resources

| Resource | Analog | Behavior |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **Resolve** | Health | Reduced by enemy “outrage,” “gotchas,” “swarm pressure.” Packs: Cheeseburgers, Gold Sneakers, Rally Energy |
| **Voice** | Ammo pool (shared or per-weapon) | Spent on ranged rhetoric; regenerates slowly at Rally Points |
| **Momentum** | Armor / combo meter | Builds with consecutive hits; depletes when hit. High Momentum = crit “ratings boost” |
| **Brand** | Meta currency | Spent between episodes on permanent upgrades |

### 5.2 Attack Styles (Rock-Paper-Scissors Layer)

Every weapon and enemy has tags. Matching tags multiplies effectiveness.

| Style Tag | Strong vs | Weak vs | Fantasy |
|-----------|-----------|---------|---------|
| **MELEE** (Close rhetoric) | Swarm, distracted foes | Ranged Speakers, Flying Drones of Discourse | In-your-face interruptions, finger-point jabs |
| **RANGED** (Projected rhetoric) | Flying, distant, boss weak points | Armored Bureaucrats up close | Mic throws, speech-bubble rockets |
| **AOE / CROWD** | Mobs, Woke Clusters | Single bosses (diluted) | Rally cheers, wall of text |
| **PIERCE / BOSS** | High-armor bosses, elites | Fast swarms (slow cast) | Deep-cut logic beams, subpoena spears |
| **CONTROL** | Chargers, enraged Karens | Immune elites | “You’re fired” stun, gavel freeze |
| **ULTIMATE** | Everything (cooldown) | Nothing (but rare) | Full Rally — screen-clear speech |

**Bosses** favor **PIERCE + CONTROL** phases.  
**Mobs** favor **AOE + MELEE**.  
**Mixed rooms** reward **loadout flexibility**.

### 5.3 Weapons (Primary Arsenal)

Each weapon has: base fire mode, alt-fire, upgrade path (3 tiers), and preferred tags.

#### W1 — **Debate Gavel** (Starter Melee)
- **Primary:** Swing arc of gavel-shockwaves (MELEE)  
- **Alt:** Slam → short stun cone (CONTROL)  
- **Upgrades:** Wider arc → chain-interrupt → “Order in the Court” ground pound  
- **Visuals:** Wooden → chrome → gold gavel trails  

#### W2 — **Mic Drop** (Starter Ranged)
- **Primary:** Lob speech-bubble grenades (RANGED)  
- **Alt:** Straight “soundbite beam” (PIERCE light)  
- **Upgrades:** Bounce bubbles → remote detonation → double mic  

#### W3 — **Framing Nailgun** (Mid unlock)
- **Primary:** Rapid “frame job” nails that tag enemies with *Narrative Vulnerability* (RANGED)  
- **Alt:** Deploy picture-frame trap (CONTROL zone)  
- **Strong vs:** Media enemies, flying cameras  
- **Upgrades:** Multi-nail → sticky frames → “Front Page” execute on tagged foes  

#### W4 — **Logic Laser** (Mid unlock)
- **Primary:** Continuous beam that shreds armor (PIERCE)  
- **Alt:** Charge shot — syllogism supernova (BOSS)  
- **Voice cost:** High  
- **Upgrades:** Heat sink → prism split → “Reductio ad Absurdum” overcharge  

#### W5 — **Facts & Figures Spreadsheet Storm** (Late mid)
- **Primary:** Shotgun burst of paper sheets (MELEE-range AOE hybrid)  
- **Alt:** Paper airplane homing volleys (RANGED)  
- **Strong vs:** Bureaucracy armor  
- **Upgrades:** Tax-form confetti → audit tornado → “Balanced Budget” shield on kill  

#### W6 — **The Wall (of Text)** (Late unlock)
- **Primary:** Deploy temporary cover barrier that also damages on contact (CONTROL + DEFENSE)  
- **Alt:** Push wall forward as a rolling billboard (AOE)  
- **Upgrades:** Taller wall → spike quotes → “Build the Wall” fortress mode  

#### W7 — **Charisma Cannon** (Episode 5+)
- **Primary:** Slow, heavy crowd-pleaser blast (AOE)  
- **Alt:** Channel Rally — regenerates Resolve while holding (support)  
- **Ultimate synergy:** Fills Momentum fast  

#### W8 — **Yuge Hands** (Secret / plaque reward)
- **Primary:** Grab-and-yeet enemies into each other (MELEE special)  
- **Alt:** Clap shockwave  
- **Status:** Rare, meme-tier, high fun  

### 5.4 Weapon Loadout
- **2 active weapons** hot-swappable (1 / 2 or scroll)  
- **1 utility** (Wall, Rally, Stun-spray “You’re Fired”)  
- Swapping mid-combo preserves Momentum briefly  

### 5.5 Enemy “Damage” Types (What Hits the Player)
- **Outrage Stacks** — DoT if ignored (Karen special)  
- **Gotcha Flash** — Instant Resolve chunk + screen glitch  
- **Swarm Pressure** — Soft CC when 4+ mobs nearby  
- **Injunction** — Temporary weapon disable (Judge minions)  
- **Spin Fog** — Reduces accuracy / beam range  

### 5.6 Boss Combat Philosophy
Bosses are **puzzle-DPS hybrids**:
1. **Telegraphed attacks** with satire flair  
2. **Phase changes** at 66% / 33% that force weapon/style swap  
3. **Add spawns** mid-fight for resource pressure  
4. **Weak-point windows** after successful CONTROL or plaque-triggered environmental interacts

## 7. Episodes / Levels

Each episode = 1–3 maps + boss arena. Doom-like: keys (Red Tie, Blue Check, Golden Escalator), locked doors, secrets (1–3 per map), exit.

### Episode Map

| Ep | Title | Setting | Themes | Boss | Est. Maps |
|----|-------|---------|--------|------|-----------|
| 0 | **The Basement of Ambition** | Queens home / workshop | Tutorial, movement, gavel + mic | None (dummy Karen) | 1 |
| 1 | **Strip Mall of Suffering** | Suburbia / HOA hell | Karens, first elite | Manager of Karens | 2 |
| 2 | **Campus of Eternal Protest** | University quad / admin | Woke Mobs, Hydra intro | Cancel Culture Hydra | 2–3 |
| 3 | **Bureau of Infinite Forms** | Brutalist offices | Bureaucrats, Autopen | Autopen | 2–3 |
| 4 | **Ballot Catacombs** | Warehouse / server farm | Fraud themes, fog | Election Fraud | 3 |
| 5 | **Tribunal of the Unelected** | Courtroom dimension | Injunctions, judges | Rogue Judge Tribunal | 2–3 |
| 6 | **Prime Time Abyss** | TV studios / control rooms | Media Leviathan | Media Leviathan | 2–3 |
| 7 | **The Swamp & The Oval** | DC monuments + White House gauntlet | All systems | The Swamp | 3 + finale |

**Post-game:** Boss Rush · New Game+ (harder spin fog, enemy modifiers) · Plaque Hunter mode.

### Level Design Rules
1. **Landmark rooms** every 60–90 seconds of traversal  
2. **At least 1 plaque** per map; 2–3 on boss-adjacent maps  
3. **Verticality light** — balconies, press boxes, escalators  
4. **Secret ratio** — ~15% of map items behind secrets  
5. **Safe Rally Points** — Voice regen + lore radio snippets  
6. **Color-coded keys** with satirical names

## 10. Art Direction

### 10.1 Chosen Approach (Hybrid) — LOCKED
**Primary: In-engine** raycaster textures, billboard sprites, VFX particles, UI.  
**Secondary: Imagine-generated micro-sprites** for enemy icons, pickup stickers, plaque ornaments, title art — **removable backgrounds**, pixel-snapped or texture stamps.

### 10.0 Rendering — LOCKED
**Classic 2.5D raycaster** (not full WebGL mesh world for v1).

### 10.2 Visual Style Keywords
- **Saturated primaries** — gold, navy, newsroom red  
- **Readable silhouettes** — big heads, exaggerated props  
- **Doom corridor bones** — concrete, danger stripes, industrial light  
- **Satire skins** — posters, chyron bars, protest signs as wall texture  
- **VFX language** — speech bubbles, gavels, paper storms, headline flashes, poll confetti  

### 10.3 Character Scale
- Player view: classic FPS arms + weapon  
- Enemies: slightly chibi-menacing (1.0–1.4× human)  
- Bosses: arena-scale props (Autopen = room-sized quill machine)

### 10.4 UI
- Health: **Resolve** bar with eagle ends  
- Ammo: **Voice** waveform meter  
- Crosshair: changes by weapon style tag  
- Plaque popup: parchment + gold frame, skippable  
- Map: wireframe “deal blueprint” aesthetic  

### 10.5 Audio Direction (Placeholder → production)
- MIDI-meets-stadium-rock combat loop  
- Karen screech stingers (comedic, not piercing-harmful)  
- Mic feedback on hit confirms  
- Boss themes: court drums, election night strings, swamp bass
