# Story Bible — Trump Doom

Companion to the full [Game Design Document](./GAME_DESIGN_DOCUMENT.md).  
Focus: narrative spine, character arcs, plaque philosophy, boss mythology.

---

## Premise

In a parallel arcade America, **Narrative Demons** have leaked out of cable boxes, campus quads, and sealed courtrooms. They feed on confusion and spin. **Donald J. Trump** — always rendered as the adult Legend of destiny — walks the mythic road to the Oval. Childhood ambition lives only on **golden plaques**. He is not “leveling up into adulthood”; he is **Doomguy of destiny** from frame one.

This is **origin myth as Doom campaign**, not biopic.

**Icon rule:** Like classical art that portrays the sacred infant as a small grown man — the player character is always suited Legend Donald.

## Protagonist Arc

| Layer | Emotional beat | Gameplay mirror |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|
| Plaques (lore) | Boyhood deals, radio prophecy | Codex only |
| In-game body | Always Legend | Suit arms, red tie |
| Power fantasy | Bigger arguments | Weapons + upgrades |
| Mandate | Player skill | True ending / cosmetics |

**Internal want:** Be the biggest.  
**External goal:** Reach the Oval.  
**Conversion fantasy:** Foes **join the Trump-Train** (never die bloody).  
**Satirical need:** Learn which arguments work on which monsters (style tags).

---

## Antagonist Mythology

Enemies are **not** real demographic groups; they are **carnival masks** of media tropes:

- **Karen** — Entitlement as a charging beast  
- **Libtard** — Caricature of online smugness as a kiting mage  
- **Woke Mob** — Swarm/hive, cancel chant as AOE  
- **Autopen** — Bureaucracy’s signature without soul  
- **Election Fraud** — Maze of doubt (puzzle boss, not a documentary claim)  
- **Rogue Judges** — Power without ballot as dungeon lords  
- **The Swamp** — Composite of all prior sins of spin  

Boss intros should always play as **arcade title cards**, not news chyrons.

## Episode Story Beats (Short Form)

1. **Basement** — Call to adventure (radio)  
2. **Strip Mall** — First taste of public combat (Karens)  
3. **Campus** — Swarm politics; identity of Framing weapon  
4. **Bureau** — Autopen; forms as walls  
5. **Catacombs** — Trust, verify, maze  
6. **Tribunal** — Silencing as a mechanic (weapon disable)  
7. **Prime Time** — Ratings as DPS metaphor  
8. **Swamp / Oval** — Synthesis boss; inauguration if plaques complete

## Plaque Writing Rules

1. First person, Donald’s mythic voice  
2. Max ~140 characters for toast; longer in codex  
3. One joke or hook per plaque  
4. Never break the fourth wall about GitHub  
5. Optional: environmental match (plaque near related enemy type)
